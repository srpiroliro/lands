# Strategy

## Top 5 Agents

### 1. Proposal Draft Agent
Creates the initial draft of the proposal and sends it to Marcus so that he can review and approve/deny it.

#### Assumptions
The pricing doc consists of [item name] -- [price]. But this would need proper review with the actual pricing doc.
Even better would be to see old proposal documents, to properly understand the pricing and better steer the models.

#### What it does?
 - Intakes the notes, project type, site photos, budget.
 - Generates the initial draft of the proposal based on the pricing sheet.
 - Creates render brief for Carlos if >30k
 - Sends the initial draft to Marcus for review.
 - On approval, sends the draft to the client for final review.

#### What it replaces or unblocks?
Taps into the 35-40% leads are lost due to slow proposal.
 
#### Estimated ROI
Potential revenue opportunity of ~$2.5M per year.

#### Why this position?
Highest ROI/bottleneck of all.

### Post-Sign Agent
Moves signed clients from "contract signed" to "ready to schedule" faster.

#### What it does?
- Tracks deposit, HOA submission, permits, and start-date readiness.
- Sends reminders to clients when deposits, HOA actions, or approvals are missing.

#### What it replaces or unblocks?
Replaces Jenna manual admin work.

### Closed-Lost Reactivation Agent
Re-engages old closed-lost leads with personalized follow-ups

#### What it does?
- Gets closed-lost leads from GHL.
- Segments by project type, budget, last conversation, and lost reason.
- Writes personalized SMS/email follow-ups.
- Passes interested replies to Brittany or Marcus.
- Tracks reactivated opportunities in GHL.

#### What it replaces or unblocks?
Replaces sporadic manual reactivation blasts.
 
#### Estimated ROI
At a 2% re-close rate on 1,400 leads, that is 28 recovered deals. At ~$28K average project value, that equals ~$784K in potential recovered revenue.

#### Why this position?

### Build Update Agent
Updates to the client on the progress of the project.

#### What it does?
- Reads Jobber check-ins and CompanyCam photo uploads.
- Summarizes daily/weekly progress in Marcus-style updates.
- Sends project updates through GHL.
- Flags delays, risks, or missing updates for Jenna.

#### What it replaces or unblocks?
Inconsistent crew texts, random CompanyCam notifications, and Marcus’s manual Loom updates. Reduces “what’s going on?” calls to Jenna
 
#### Estimated ROI
Unknown. Saves Jenna time by reducing customer calls and improves the overall customer experience (referrals and premium positioning).

#### Why this position?
Due to unknown ROI

## Why is your #1 choice the #1, and not the founder's stated #1 priority?
If by "Speed up quoting", all quoting is meant (proposal + crew price mismatch + ...), then the priorities do not match (although it is still covered by the Proposal Agent and Pricing Agent).

If only drafts are meant, then the priorities match.

## What is one agent you considered but did not include in your top 5, and why not?
Lead Qualifier Agent. It would save a small amount of time per week (1-2 hrs/week). 
My idea is that the Pricing Agent actually saves more time and money.
